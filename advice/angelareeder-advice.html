Hi my name is Angela and I have some advice for you!

It's okay to step back!

Sometimes you will get to a point where you feel like you are banging your head against a wall.

When this happens its okay to stop and take a break. Even if it is a few days. When you come back your mind will feel clearer and it will be easier to see the problem.

Remember if something is off its something you have done along the way. Its okay to go back and double check your work.

It's okay to ask for help or look to see if someone else has had this issue and how they solved the problem.

This is a normal part of learning. Its learning by seeing and learning by doing.
